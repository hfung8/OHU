Water

We partnered with an international NGO who raised the resources to construct a new borehole to provide clean, accessible water to the orphanage and the surrounding community. Contractors drilled to the depth of 82 meters to find clean, potable water. Finding water was a daily concern for women and children of the village. Access to clean water greatly improves health and has a large impact on the alleviation of poverty.
