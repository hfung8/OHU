Mission and Core Values

We believe every child deserves a strong and positive foundation for their future. We will provide complete care to the most vulnerable children in our area and aim to break the devastating cycle of poverty, illiteracy, and inequality.

Core Values

We want our children to move beyond mere survival. We are committed to a comprehensive care approach for orphaned and abandoned children.

We work hard to make sure the needs of our community are always our primary consideration.

We aim to break the devastating cycle of poverty, illiteracy, and inequality.

Through love, counseling, and education our children can have bright futures and lead the change for the next generation.
