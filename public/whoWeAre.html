Orphans of Hope Uganda (OHU)

Orphans of Hope Uganda (OHU) is a registered non-profit organization based in Central Uganda. OHU was created in 2012 in response to the urgent need for action in caring for abandoned children. Our goal is to give vulnerable children a family atmosphere, within a loving and safe environment. Despite their struggles, our vision is to watch our orphans grow to be well-adjusted, happy, and productive members of society. We provide a happy home, healthy food, medical care, and an education.
